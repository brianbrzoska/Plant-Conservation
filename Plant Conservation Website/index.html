Why do we save Plants?

  • If there are no plants, there is no humanity. Plants are vital to our lives and essential to our survival. Plants clean air, water, soil, and provide clothes, food, and medicine.
  • Plants hold an immeasurable value because of the beauty, tranquility, and the escape from our chaotic world. Integral to the world's ecosystem, plants and their incredible diversity make life as we know it liveable.
